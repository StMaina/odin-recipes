PANCAKES RECIPE

Back to home

Description

Follow these steps to whip up some badman pancakes!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • milk
  • sugar
  • vanilla
  • butter

Steps

  1. mix flour, eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla in a bowl
  2. heat butter in a pan
  3. pour a ladle of the mixture into the pan
  4. cook for 2 minutes on each side
